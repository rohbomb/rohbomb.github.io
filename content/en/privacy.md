# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

Welcome to TIKKLES (tikklabs.com). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, ensuring compliance with global standards including Google AdSense policies.

## 1. Information We Collect
We do not directly collect sensitive personally identifiable information (such as names or emails) as we do not require account registration. However, when you visit our blog, our servers and third-party tools automatically collect some basic usage data:
- IP addresses, browser types, and device information.
- Service usage records, including pages visited and time spent.

## 2. Cookies and Web Beacons
TIKKLES uses "cookies" to store information about visitors' preferences, to evaluate website performance, and to deliver personalized content.
- **Google AdSense & Advertising Cookies**: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.
- **Google's use of advertising cookies** enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
- **Opting Out**: Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## 3. Third-Party Services
To improve our content and monetize the blog, we work with third-party service providers that may set their own cookies:
- **Google Analytics**: We use this to analyze website traffic and user behavior. The data collected is anonymized.
- **Google AdSense**: Used for displaying advertisements.

## 4. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)
Depending on your geographic location (e.g., the EU or California), you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. Because we do not store personal profiles, the most effective way to exercise your data rights—such as opting out of data collection—is to manage your browser's cookie settings or use third-party opt-out tools.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to refer to our About page.
