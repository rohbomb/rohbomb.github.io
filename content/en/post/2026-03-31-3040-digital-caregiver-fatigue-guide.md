## Core Problem 1. Purifying the Algorithm-Trapped YouTube Ecosystem

The place where parents spend the most time on their smartphones is YouTube. However, a single accidentally clicked sensational 'Shorts' or fake news video often leads to an endless bombardment of algorithm recommendations. 90% of the complaints saying "YouTube got weird" are due to this algorithmic contamination.

The most definitive way to solve this is to **completely hide 'YouTube Shorts,' which is the biggest culprit, from the screen.**
